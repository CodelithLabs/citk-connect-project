**CITK Connect** is a hyper-localized student utility app designed to solve the three biggest challenges newcomers face at the Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar: **Navigation Anxiety, Academic Confusion, and Safety.**

It acts as a "Digital Senior," guiding students from their first day on campus to their final exams.
